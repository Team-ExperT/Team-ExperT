About

VicRun is a fun and challenging game for children where they can help cleaning up garbage and water pollution based on the water quality.

It is completed with attractive animation videos to develop knowledge about saving environment.

VicRun uses an actual waterway water quality data from Melbourne Water to depict the actual water quality into game difficulty level.

Less cleaner the water, more challenging the game will be.

VicRun is designed as an ultimate solution for parents to have a mobile game for their children that at the same time gives knowledge about environmental protection.
