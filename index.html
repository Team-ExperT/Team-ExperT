About

VicRun is a fun and challenging game for children where they can help cleaning up garbage and water pollution around Melbourne area.

It is completed with attractive animation videos to develop knowledge about saving environment.

VicRun uses an actual waterway water quality data from Melbourne Water to bring an awareness about water quality through representation of the real world into game environment.

VicRun is designed as an ultimate solution for parents to have a proper mobile game for their children.
